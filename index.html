How to make simple pasta:

Ingredients Quantity
Dry Pasta 2 boxes
Pasta Sauce 1 Jar
Water 3 cups
Onion & Garlic 5
Veggies! 4

Steps:

  • Pour the pasta on the bottom of pot or pan
  • Add the remaining ingredients
  • Bring the mixture to a boil, then simmer until pasta is al- dente
  • Finally serve and enjoy

    • The ultimate homemade burger

    Ingredients Quantity
    Ground Beef 1 pound
    Dale's seasoning 3 tablespoon
    burger buns 1
    burger topings
  • tomatoe
  • lettuce
  • bacon
  • onions
  • ketchup
    • (any amount)

    Steps:

  • grind the beef - you don't want the meet to be clunky
  • Don't overwork your meet - this will make it tough and dense
  • shape patties - wider buns make for better burgers
  • make an indetantion - in the center to prevent it from plumping up in the center
  • Don't season too learly - salt changes the struvture of proteins and toughens burgers so don't season your ground beef until you have formed your patties and are ready to grill
  • Get a goo sear - Once on the grill, let patties brown and sear well before flipping, and do not press on the burger
