How to make simple pasta:

Ingredients Quantity
Dry Pasta 2 boxes
Pasta Sauce 1 Jar
Water 3 cups
Onion & Garlic 5
Veggies! 4

Step 1:

  • Pour the pasta on the bottom of pot or pan
  • Add the remaining ingredients
  • Bring the mixture to a boil, then simmer until pasta is al- dente
  • Finally serve and enjoy
